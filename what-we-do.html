Our Services

We offer a diverse range of high-tech services and supplies designed to meet the rigorous demands of the modern energy industry.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Solutions

Our HybriDenSafe system is a scenario-based video analytics solution designed for robust monitoring of industrial operations. It features over 20 pre-built, configurable AI modules to cover 90% of common use cases, enhancing hazard detection by 7x and enabling 24/7 automated site monitoring.

Pipeline Inspection Technology

MTM-G Technology

This non-contact solution monitors the real condition of pipelines by measuring their magnetic field strength. It effectively locates stress concentration areas, assessing danger levels for aboveground, underground, and underwater pipelines without service interruption.

Electro Scan Leak Detection

Using electrical current, this technology accurately detects and measures all pipe defects, including those hidden in joints and cracks. It rapidly pinpoints the exact location and size of leaks, and can even identify illegal water pipe connections.

Asset Integrity Management

Our primary tool for Asset Integrity Management is Motion Amplification Vibration Analysis. This revolutionary technology uses video to detect and visualize microscopic movements, allowing us to assess structural health and identify potential faults in complex equipment and piping without physical contact.

HSE Safety Equipment Rental

We provide cost-effective and flexible rental solutions for critical safety equipment, backed by strong local support and a commitment to compliance. Our offerings include personal gas detectors and Emergency Escape Breathing Devices (EEBD).

Valve Supply

Through our partnership with renowned manufacturers like Trust Valve, we provide a premium selection of valves and fittings engineered for the most demanding environments in the energy sector, including Ball, Butterfly, Gate, and Globe valves.

Manpower Supply

We provide highly skilled and specialized manpower to support the operational needs of our clients. Our commitment to personnel development ensures we maintain a motivated and competent workforce ready to tackle the challenges of the industry.
