Legasi Futura Sdn. Bhd. (LFSB) is a solution provider company in the energy sector, based in Miri, Sarawak. Founded by Sarawakian Bumiputera individuals, we proudly hold a 100% local ownership and possess a valuable Petronas license. Our primary objective is to provide innovative and cutting-edge solutions within various industries. With a fresh presence in the market, we specialize in equipment and material trading, as well as maintenance and inspection services for pipelines, tanks, and vessels.

Our commitment to excellence is unwavering. We are dedicated to staying at the forefront of technological advancements, ensuring that we consistently offer the most advanced and efficient solutions to our valued customers. By embracing the latest technologies available, we empower our clients with solutions that enhance safety, productivity, and sustainability across their operations.

One of our core strengths is our focus on personnel development. We recognize that our people are our most valuable asset. Therefore, we invest in continuous training and professional growth opportunities for our team members. This commitment to our people ensures that we maintain a skilled and motivated workforce that is equipped to take on the challenges of the future.

At Legasi Futura, we are more than just a service provider. We are a trusted partner, working closely with our clients to understand their unique requirements and deliver tailored solutions that exceed expectations. Our commitment to building long-term relationships and delivering exceptional results has earned us a solid reputation in the industry.
